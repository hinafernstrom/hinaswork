Jay Fai

2023 --- Custom logo type, packaging design, zine

JAY FAI is is a Michelin rated chief from Bangkok Thailand known for her delicacies in Thai cuisine. This branding project aims to visualize the vibrant colors and flavors in Thai street food culture. The layered logo symbolizes the layers of flavors and spices within her dishes and takes inspiration from Thai textiles and street vendor tents.
